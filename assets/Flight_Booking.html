Frequently Asked Questions:

  1. How do I book a flight on your website?

    1. Booking a flight is easy! Simply visit our homepage and use the flight search tool. Enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Our platform will display a list of available flights for you to choose from. Select your preferred flight, provide passenger details, and proceed to payment.

  2. What payment methods do you accept for flight bookings?

    1. We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and online payment platforms. You can choose the payment option that's most convenient for you during the checkout process.

  3. Can I cancel or change my flight after booking?

    1. Yes, you can! We understand that plans can change. Depending on the fare type you've selected, you may be able to make changes to your booking or cancel it. Keep in mind that change and cancellation policies vary based on the airline and fare rules. Make sure to review the terms and conditions associated with your booking or contact our customer support for assistance.

  4. How do I retrieve my e-ticket after booking?

    1. After completing your booking, you will receive a confirmation email containing your e-ticket details. You can also log in to your account on our website to view and print your e-ticket. Remember to bring a copy of your e-ticket and a valid ID to the airport for check-in.
